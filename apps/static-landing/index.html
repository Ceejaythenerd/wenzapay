Frequently asked questions

Is WenzaPay non-custodial?

Yes, for EVM chains we use XPUB derivation meaning we never hold your private keys. For Solana, we use a temporary custodial forwarder to automatically sweep funds to your cold wallet.

What chains are supported?

We currently support Ethereum, Polygon, Tron, and Solana natively.

How does the fiat off-ramp work?

We partner with Bridge.xyz to instantly swap received stablecoins and wire the USD equivalent directly to your corporate bank account.

Do you handle KYC?

Yes, our embedded Persona integration handles merchant onboarding and high-risk customer verification automatically.
